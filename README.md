<h1 align="center">Minecraft Datapack: <br> Craft Spawners and selected Monster Eggs</h1>

# Features

- Craft spawners with totems of undying and experience bottles.
- Craft spawn eggs for zombies, husks, drowned, zombie villagers, skeletons, strays, bogged, parched, wither skeletons, piglins, witches and creepers.
- The recipes are survival friendly and require difficult to acquire (at least in bulk) materials such as mob heads, which are used as the basis for all the recipes.
